Geopolitics Centre ✓ Verified Reuters BBC News
NATO member states have announced coordinated military drills across three Baltic nations following an incident in which Russian aircraft entered Finnish airspace without authorisation. The move is widely seen as a direct response to what Western officials are calling a sustained pattern of deliberate provocation from Moscow.
Key Points
Russian aircraft crossed into Finnish airspace triggering NATO response protocols
Joint military exercises announced across Estonia, Latvia and Lithuania
Defence ministers convening in Brussels for emergency talks today
Russia has not publicly responded to the allegations as of publishing
